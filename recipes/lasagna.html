Homemade Lasagna

The final product, delictious homemade lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 28oz can crushed tomatos
  • 1 8oz can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 16oz package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 16oz package shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients
  2. Step 2: Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Step 3: Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes
  4. Step 4: Sitr in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reuce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes
  5. Step 5: While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside. While the noodles are cooking, preheat oven to 375.
  6. Step 6: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt and pepper in a large bowl until combined
  7. Step 7: Assemble lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover
  8. Step 8: Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the remaining noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil
  9. Step 9: Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  10. Step 10: Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
  11. Step 11: Enjoy!
